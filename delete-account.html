Money Trail — Account Deletion

Use this page to request deletion of your Money Trail account and associated cloud data.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us from your registered sign-in email at: YOUR SUPPORT EMAIL
  2. Subject: Delete my Money Trail account
  3. Include: your sign-in email and (optional) reason

What gets deleted

  • Your Money Trail account identity used for cloud sync.
  • Synced cloud data stored in our backend (Supabase), associated with your account.

What is not deleted automatically

  • Data stored locally on your device (delete by clearing app storage or uninstalling).
  • Google Drive backup files stored in your own Drive (delete them from Google Drive).

Timeline

We typically process deletion requests within 7–30 days.
