1) Who we are

Money Trail is built and operated by Sam (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

2) Summary

  • Offline-first: your finance data is stored on your device by default.
  • Cloud Sync is optional and Pro-only. If enabled, we sync data to support multi-device restore and shared profiles.
  • Google Drive backup is optional. If enabled, the app uploads a backup file to your Google Drive (app-private storage).
  • We use Firebase Analytics/Crashlytics to understand performance and fix crashes.
  • Free users may see ads via Google AdMob (depending on your build and settings).
  • We do not sell your personal information.

3) Information we collect

The information we collect depends on which features you use.

A) Data you enter in the app

  • Transactions (amount, date/time, category, notes/description, payment mode).
  • Accounts, budgets, debts, goals, tags, rules, and settings.
  • Optional attachments/receipts (only if you add them).

B) Account & authentication data

  • Email address and basic profile info from Google Sign-In (for sign-in and enabled services).
  • Authentication identifiers/tokens required to keep you signed in.

C) Cloud Sync & Shared Profiles data (Pro-only, optional)

  • If you enable Cloud Sync, your finance data may be uploaded to our backend to sync across devices and support shared profiles.
  • Shared profiles may allow invited/accepted members to access data inside that shared profile (depending on role/permissions).

D) Google Drive backup data (optional)

  • If enabled, the app uploads a backup file to your Google Drive in an app-private storage area. You can revoke Drive access in your Google Account settings.

E) Device, usage & diagnostics data

  • App version, device model, OS version, language/locale.
  • Crash reports and diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Basic usage events (Firebase Analytics), if enabled.

F) Advertising data (free users, if ads are enabled)

  • Advertising ID (where available), IP address (approximate location), ad impressions/clicks, and diagnostics.
  • Ad delivery and measurement signals (personalization depends on your device/region settings).

4) How we use information

  • Provide core features like tracking, reports, and insights.
  • Authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • Sync and restore your data across devices (Cloud Sync) when enabled (Pro-only).
  • Enable shared profiles when Cloud Sync is enabled.
  • Back up and restore your data to Google Drive when enabled.
  • Fix crashes and improve performance (Crashlytics).
  • Improve features using usage insights (Analytics).
  • Show and measure ads for free users (AdMob), if ads are enabled.

5) Legal bases

Depending on your location, we process information under one or more of these legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract (to provide requested features like sync/backup).
  • Consent (for optional features like Cloud Sync/Drive backup and certain analytics settings where required).
  • Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, product improvement).
  • Legal obligations (compliance with applicable law).

6) Where your data is stored

  • On-device storage: your finance data is stored locally on your phone by default.
  • Cloud Sync (Supabase): if you enable Cloud Sync (Pro-only), your finance data may be uploaded to our backend to sync across devices and support shared profiles.
  • Google Drive backup: if you enable Drive backup, the app may store a backup file in your Google Drive under your account (app-private storage). You can revoke Drive access any time in your Google Account settings.

7) Sharing & disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data in these cases:

  • Service providers that help run enabled features (authentication, sync/hosting, crash reporting, analytics, notifications).
  • Advertising partners for ads and measurement (AdMob) when ads are enabled for free users.
  • Shared profiles (only if you enable Cloud Sync and invite/accept members).
  • When you export/share reports or files yourself.
  • To comply with law, enforce rights, or prevent fraud/abuse.

Common third-party services

  • Google Sign-In (authentication)
  • Google Drive API (backup/restore when enabled)
  • Supabase (Cloud Sync backend when enabled)
  • Firebase Analytics (usage analytics)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting/diagnostics)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (optional notifications/reminders)
  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK (ads for free users, if enabled)

8) International transfers

Some service providers may process data on servers in various countries. When this happens, we rely on appropriate safeguards and provider security practices.

9) Data retention

  • On-device data stays until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall.
  • If Cloud Sync is enabled, cloud data may remain until you delete it in the app or request deletion.
  • Crash/diagnostic logs are retained for a limited period for debugging.
  • Google Drive backup files remain in your Drive until you delete them.

10) Security

We use reasonable safeguards such as encryption in transit (HTTPS) for network communications. No system is 100% secure, but we work to reduce risk.

11) Your choices & rights

  • Use the app offline without enabling Cloud Sync.
  • Enable/disable Cloud Sync in Settings (Pro-only).
  • Enable/disable Google Drive backup in Settings and revoke access in your Google Account settings.
  • Export your data using in-app export tools (if available).
  • Delete local data by deleting records, clearing app storage, or uninstalling.
  • Request cloud/account deletion by contacting support. We may ask you to verify account ownership before completing deletion.
  • Ads: limit ad personalization via Android privacy settings (availability depends on device/region).

12) Children’s privacy

Money Trail is not designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

13) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date and may notify you within the app where appropriate.
