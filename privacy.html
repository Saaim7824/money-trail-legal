Money Trail — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 Dec 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Money Trail (“the App”) operated by Sam, collects, uses, and shares information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Data you enter: transactions, accounts, budgets, debts, goals, notes, tags, and optional attachments.
  • Account data (Google Sign-In): email, name, and profile picture (if available) for authentication and sync.
  • Diagnostics & analytics (Firebase): app performance, crash logs, and basic usage events.
  • Advertising data (AdMob for free users): advertising ID (where available), IP address (approximate location), and ad interactions.

How we use information

  • Provide app features and account authentication.
  • Sync data (Supabase) and back up/restore data (Google Drive) when enabled.
  • Monitor crashes/performance and improve the app (Firebase services).
  • Serve and measure ads for free users (AdMob).

Where your data is stored

  • On-device: stored locally for offline use.
  • Supabase (optional sync): stored in our backend when sync is enabled.
  • Google Drive (optional backup): backup files stored in your Drive when enabled.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Service providers needed to operate the app (e.g., Supabase, Firebase, Google APIs).
  • Advertising partners (AdMob) for ads in the free version.
  • Authorities if required by law or to prevent fraud/abuse.

Data retention

  • Local data remains until you delete it or uninstall.
  • Synced data remains until you delete it in-app or request deletion.
  • Drive backups remain until you delete them from your Google Drive.

Your choices

  • Export or delete your data in-app (where available).
  • Revoke permissions from Android Settings.
  • Limit ad personalization in Android privacy settings (availability varies).
